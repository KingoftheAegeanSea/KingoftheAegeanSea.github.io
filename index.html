A little about me

I am a high school student who will be getting my diploma by the end of the year 2026 and have various skills in Computer Networking. I am pursuing a CCNA certification, AWS Cloud Practitioner, Comptia Security+ and a AWS Certified AI Practitioner certification. I have experience going to other countries like the country of Antigua and Barbuda where I went to schools of different standings from rural schools to the school funded by the prime minister (of Antigua and Barbuda) himself and myself along with a couple of other highly skilled individuals troubleshooted and created networks that function throughout the sites. I am also on the verge of earning an Associates at Bellevue College before the end of 2026. I also have experience in both Java, C++, and HTML as both a coder and a teacher for local studetns through a Bellevue School District Funded Program.

The following labs are hands on problems that I personally solved from standing up multiprotocol routing topologies, hardening switches against common attacks, configuring site to site and remote access VPNs on both Fortinet and Palo Alto platforms, and documenting every step clearly enough that someone else could pick it up and follow along.

I have taken the CCNA course and acquired a Credly badge proving my successful completion of the course, and I'm currently looking for opportunities to put that foundation to work on a real network team.
